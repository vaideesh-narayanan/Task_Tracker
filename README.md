# Task Tracker

A simple and offline-first task tracking application to help you keep track of your daily goals, pending work, and completed tasks effectively.

## Features

- **Fully Offline**: No internet required.
- **Privacy Focused**: All data is stored only on your phone. Developers, Google, or any third-party don't have access to your data.
- **No Unnecessary Permissions**: This app doesn't ask you for any permissions except to send notifications about your tasks.
- **Organization**: Filter tasks by All, Pending, Completed, Expired, and Archived.
- **Themes & Customization**: Supports light and dark modes, with a choice of 10 modern accent colors.
- **Backup & Restore**: Easily backup your tasks and restore them on demand.
- **Recycle Bin**: Recover accidentally deleted tasks easily.
